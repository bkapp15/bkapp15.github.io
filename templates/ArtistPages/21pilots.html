Twenty One Pilots

American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. The group originally consisted of lead vocalist Tyler Joseph, bassist Nick Thomas, and drummer Chris Salih. Thomas and Salih left the group in 2011 and drummer Josh Dun joined. The group currently consists of Joseph and Dun.
