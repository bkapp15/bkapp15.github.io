Beyonce Houston, Texas 1997 R&B, Pop American singer, songwriter, and actress born and raised in Houston, Texas. She got her start as lead singer of girl-group Destiny's Child and then went on to have a successful solo career.
Twenty One Pilots Columbus, Ohio 2009 Alternative hip hop, Electropop American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. The group originally consisted of lead vocalist Tyler Joseph, bassist Nick Thomas, and drummer Chris Salih. Thomas and Salih left the group in 2011 and drummer Josh Dun joined. The group currently consists of Joseph and Dun.
Rihanna Saint Michael, Barbados 2003 R&B, Pop Barbadian singer, songwriter, and actress. She got her start in the industry by releasing demo tapes and auditioning for Jay Z. After signing, she began her solo career.
The Chainsmokers New York City, New York 2012 Electronic dance music, Pop American DJ/producer duo from New York City. The group consists of Andrew Taggart and Alex Pall.
Ed Sheeran Halifax, England 2004 Pop, Acoustic English singer-songwriter and record producer. His independently released EP caught the attention of Elton John and Jamie Foxx. He then signed with Asylum Records and released his debut album.
Bruno Mars Los Angeles, California 2004 R&B, Funk American singer-songwriter, instrumentalist, record producer, and choreographer. He began making music and performing at a young age and eventually moved to Los Angeles to pursue a musical career. There he signed with Atlantic Records.
