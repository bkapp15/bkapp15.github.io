24k Magic | by Bruno Mars

“24K Magic” is a braggadocious party anthem described by Bruno Mars as an “invitation to the party” of his third album of the same name. It combines modern-referencing lyrics (like “#blessed”) with 80s-style production helmed by Shampoo Press & Curl and The Stereotypes.
