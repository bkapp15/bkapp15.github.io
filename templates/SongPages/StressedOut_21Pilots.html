Stressed Out | by Twenty One Pilots

“Stressed Out” alternates between the point of view of the character Blurryface (a representation of Tyler’s insecurities) and the anxious Tyler feeling intense nostalgia about his childhood. It’s about a wish to regain the innocence of childhood, which existed before insecurity and money troubles became a problem.
