Work from Home | by Fifth Harmony

The infectious lead single from 5H’s 7/27, “Work From Home” is a bouncy pop song about sex. The girls are lonely at home while their significant others are stuck at work in the night. They ache for their lover’s touch, telling them they don’t have to go to work but instead stay home and “work” in bed, comparing their intimacy as a vacation.
