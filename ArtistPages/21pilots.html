21 Pilots

Origin : Columbus, Ohio

Debut : 2009

Genre : Alternative Hip Hop, Electropop

Description: American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. The group originally consisted of lead vocalist Tyler Joseph, bassist Nick Thomas, and drummer Chris Salih. Thomas and Salih left the group in 2011 and drummer Josh Dun joined. The group currently consists of Joseph and Dun.

Label : Fueled by Ramen
