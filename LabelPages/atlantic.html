Atlantic Records | The Home of Choice for Legendary Artists

Atlantic Records was founded in 1947 by Ahmet Ertegun and Herb Abramson, both avid music fans and record enthusiasts. Some of their most successful artists include: Aretha Franklin, Led Zeppelin, Ray Charles, The Rolling Stones, Cher, Cream, Coldplay, Ed Sheeran and many more.
