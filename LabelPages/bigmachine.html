Big Machine Records | Independent Country and Pop

Founded in 2005, Big Machine initially became a joint venture between Scott Borchetta and country singer Toby Keith. Taylor Swift is the label's most popular artist. Others include Tim McGraw, Reba McEntire, and Rascal Flatts.
